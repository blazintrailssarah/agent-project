## Weather Analysis Assignment (Assignment 6)

### Most Significant Weather Trend Observed

The most significant weather trend observed in the 2022 growing season (April-October) for Discovery Bay, CA (zip code 94514) was the **relatively dry conditions throughout the growing season**. Key findings include:

- **Extended Dry Periods**: Multiple dry spells lasting 7+ consecutive days with less than 1mm of precipitation
- **Lower-than-Average Monthly Precipitation**: Monthly precipitation totals were consistently below historical averages for the period
- **Heat Spikes**: Several days with maximum temperatures exceeding 35°C, indicating heat stress conditions
- **Cumulative Precipitation Deficit**: The total precipitation for the growing season was significantly below expected levels

This dry trend has important implications for agriculture in the region, potentially affecting crop yields, irrigation requirements, and water resource management.
